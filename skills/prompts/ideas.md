name: ideas

List, rate, develop, and track ideas generated by /reflect deep.

Usage:
- `/ideas` — list all novel ideas, ranked by score
- `/ideas list [status]` — filter by status (new, developing, tested, confirmed, abandoned)
- `/ideas view <id>` — full details of an idea
- `/ideas rate <id> <1-5>` — rate an idea
- `/ideas note <id> <text>` — add a note
- `/ideas develop <id>` — use LLM to expand and ground an idea
- `/ideas status <id> <status>` — update lifecycle status
