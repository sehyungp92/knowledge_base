**Key synthesis decisions:**
- **Current State** traces the temporal arc from late 2024 baseline → WHAM (Feb 2025, scaling laws + coherence extension) → AdaWorld (Mar 2025, action labeling bottleneck dissolved) → Genie 3 / Dreamer 4 (Aug 2025, implicit modeling + RL in imagination breakthroughs)
- **Capabilities** organized by maturity tier (narrow production → demo), not a flat list
- **Limitations** divided into blocking vs. significant, with the serving economics and memory architecture foregrounded as the deployment-critical constraints
- **Bottlenecks** organized by resolution horizon (1–2 years vs. 3–5 years), with the memory extension bottleneck highlighted as the highest-leverage near-term target
- **Contradictions** surface the implicit vs. explicit representation debate, the action diversity degradation at scale, and the physics coherence vs. user control tension — none of which appear in the raw data as explicit contradictions but emerge from cross-reading the limitations and breakthroughs
- **Research Opportunities** derived from the bottlenecks + the identified gaps in compound capability (AdaWorld + Genie 3 together not yet tested)
