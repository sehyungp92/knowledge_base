## Overview

Training a model (typically via SFT or other loss functions) on outputs generated by a stronger teacher model to transfer capabilities to the student model.

## Key Findings

1. DeepSeek's MoE architecture uses both shared experts (run on every token, dense) and routed experts (only top-K activated per token, sparse). (from "How DeepSeek Changes the LLM Story")
2. DeepSeek V3 has 671 billion total parameters but only 37 billion are activated for any given token due to its sparse mixture of experts architecture. (from "How DeepSeek Changes the LLM Story")
3. GPT-4 dropped from $30 per million tokens to $2 per million tokens in approximately 18 months. (from "State of Startups and AI 2025 - Sarah Guo, Conviction")
4. DeepSeek V3 is an open weights model but does not release training code, limiting the open-source community's ability to replicate the training process. (from "How DeepSeek Changes the LLM Story")
5. Windsurf is being acquired by OpenAI for $3 billion. (from "State of Startups and AI 2025 - Sarah Guo, Conviction")
6. Triple Bite had to spend years building proprietary software and label datasets for technical evaluation before applying machine learning (from "Startup Ideas You Can Now Build With AI")
7. DeepSeek V3 is a pre-training innovation whose advances come from a combination of architectural changes, parallelization improvements, and quantization techniques rather than any single breakthrough. (from "How DeepSeek Changes the LLM Story")
8. DeepSeek R1 achieves reasoning capability via a simple REINFORCE-based RL loop using symbolic verifiers (regex for math correctness, unit tests for code), without learned reward models. (from "How DeepSeek Changes the LLM Story")
9. DeepSeek is a small company of fewer than 200 employees, spun off from a Chinese hedge fund. (from "How DeepSeek Changes the LLM Story")
10. The fp8 E4M3 format cannot represent the number 17 and many other values, illustrating the severe precision loss at this quantization level. (from "How DeepSeek Changes the LLM Story")
11. DeepSeek V3 uses fp8 quantization for feed-forward matrix multiplications, converting down to fp8 before multiplication, accumulating back to fp32, and storing in bf16. (from "How DeepSeek Changes the LLM Story")
12. Waymo took approximately 10 years to go from a working demo to a paid commercial product at city scale. (from "No Priors Ep. 80 | With Andrej Karpathy from OpenAI and Tesla")
13. Cursor grew from $1M to $100M ARR in 12 months with half a million developers and zero salespeople. (from "State of Startups and AI 2025 - Sarah Guo, Conviction")
14. The Verification Principle states that an AI system can create and maintain knowledge only to the extent that it can verify that knowledge itself. (from "RL backlog: OpenAI's many RLs, clarifying distillation, and latent reasoning")
15. Deep Research was trained using end-to-end reinforcement learning on hard browsing and reasoning tasks across a range of domains. (from "RL backlog: OpenAI's many RLs, clarifying distillation, and latent reasoning")
