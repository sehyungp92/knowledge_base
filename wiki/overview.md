## Landscape Structure

The landscape model tracks six entity types per theme:

1. **Capabilities** — what AI can do, with maturity levels (experimental → emerging → production → commodity)
2. **Limitations** — what AI cannot do or does poorly, with severity and trajectory
3. **Bottlenecks** — fundamental obstacles blocking progress, with resolution horizons
4. **Breakthroughs** — significant advances that shift the landscape, with implication cascades
5. **Anticipations** — trackable predictions about future developments
6. **Cross-theme implications** — how developments in one area affect others
