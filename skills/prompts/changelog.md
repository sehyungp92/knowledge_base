View what changed in the landscape model over time.

Usage: `/changelog [theme] [Nd]`

Examples:
- `/changelog` — global changelog, last 30 days
- `/changelog autonomous_agents` — changes for a specific theme
- `/changelog 7d` — global, last 7 days
- `/changelog robotics 14d` — theme-scoped, last 14 days
