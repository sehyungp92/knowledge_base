Generate prioritised reading recommendations from coverage gaps.

Combines blind spot bottlenecks, unvalidated limitations, belief gaps,
untested anticipations, and low-coverage themes into a ranked queue
with search terms.

Usage:
- `/next` — top 5 recommendations across all themes
- `/next 10` — top 10 recommendations
- `/next robotics` — recommendations for a specific theme
- `/next 3 scaling` — 3 recommendations for scaling theme
